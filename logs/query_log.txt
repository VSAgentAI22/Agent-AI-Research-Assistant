What is Retrieval-Augmented Generation?

Retrieval-Augmented Generation is a technique that combines language generation with retrieval of existing text. This approach involves training a model to generate new text based on some input prompt or data, while also drawing upon stored knowledge from a vast corpus of texts. The retrieved text serves as an augmentation to the generated text, providing additional context, nuance, and detail.
